UK SKILLED WORKER VISA

Magnific IT Consulting Provides a Certificate Of Sponsorship to get UK Skilled Worker Visa (Tier2 Visa) Work Permit for the UK.

This Work permit visa is initially for 3years and you can extend 2 more years i.e. a total of 5 years. So that you can eligible for ILR (Indefinite Leave to Remain) i.e. PR(Permanent Resident). Skilled Worker Visa has replaced the Tier2 (General) work visa. A Skilled Wok visa legalizes you to come and stay in the UK to do an eligible job with an approved Employer. The UK is looking for skilled professionals to work in the UK under the Tier2 Visa Program.

UK Skilled Worker Visa formerly Tier2 visa categorized into four subcategories:
Tier2 General Visa:

This Tier2 general visa is for those who have a job offer in the UK and whose profession appears on the shortage occupations list. And from Jan 2021 the Tier2 General visa changed to Skilled Worker Visa.

CANADA STUDENT VISA

Study in Canada:

Canada issues study permits to more than 140,000 students from all around the world each year. If you wish to study in Canada, you must apply for a study permit. A study permit allows you to study in Canada at an approved institute for the duration of the specified course or program. A Temporary Resident Visa is also required if the student is a citizen of a country from which Temporary Resident Visas are required for entry into Canada.

Advantages of Studying in Canada

It is possible to work in Canada, while you are here as a student, on an off-campus work permit for a maximum of 20 hours a week.
